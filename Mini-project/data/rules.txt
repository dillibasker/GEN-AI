General Campus Rules

Smoking, consumption of alcohol, and use of narcotic substances are strictly prohibited inside the college campus.

Students must keep the campus clean and avoid littering. Use of plastic is discouraged as part of environmental responsibility.

All students must comply with notices and circulars issued by the college administration from time to time.

Leave and Permission Rules

Students who wish to take leave must apply in advance through the proper channel. In case of emergencies, information must be given to the class advisor as soon as possible.

Medical leave requires submission of a valid medical certificate. Unauthorized leave may affect attendance percentage.

Use of College Facilities

College facilities such as laboratories, computer centers, and sports grounds must be used responsibly.

Any damage to equipment or property must be reported immediately. Students may be required to compensate for damages caused due to negligence.
